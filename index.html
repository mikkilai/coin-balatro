How to Play

Goal: beat the score target of every Blind across 8 Antes. Each Ante has a Small Blind, Big Blind, and a rule-twisting Boss Blind.

Flip (hotkey F) tosses every coin in your purse — you get 4 Flips per round. After a flip, click coins to select them and Reroll (R) just those — 3 Rerolls per round. When you like the result, Score (S) it.

Scoring: the heads/tails pattern sets base Chips × Mult, then each coin adds its own Chips/Mult, then your Charms pile on. Watch the live preview in the sidebar.

PatternHow to get itChips × Mult

The Mint (shop, between rounds): buy Charms (passive modifiers, max 5), new coins (purse max 8), and Omens that permanently level up a pattern's base Chips and Mult.

The Crucible: melt a coin to remove it from your purse for +$1 (once per shop, min 3 coins). A thinner purse hits all-Heads and all-Tails patterns far more often — fewer, better coins is a real strategy.

Money: beating a Blind pays its reward, +$1 per unused Flip, and interest ($1 per $5 held, capped at $5).
